About the artist

Interested by comics and animation since childhood, Josyanne Cloutier graduated in visual arts, then in multimedia integration where she got captivated by website development. In 2018, she pursued studies in comic art, in order to explore storytelling in art and to realize an old dream: becoming a cartoonist. After 14 years as a front-end developer in the field of relational marketing, she decided to try her luck with a creative profession by offering narrative illustration and writing services.
